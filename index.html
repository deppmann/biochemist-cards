About This Project

This gallery was created by 750 students in BIOL 3030 (Biochemistry) at the University of Virginia as a 15-minute extra credit assignment. Each student researched a biochemist and then used AI image generators to create Studio Ghibli-style trading cards celebrating their contributions to science.

The goal was twofold: help students learn about the scientists behind the discoveries they study, and give them hands-on experience with AI prompting and image generation. In a large lecture class, projects like this help build community and make the material more personal.

How It Works

  1. Students research their assigned biochemist using AI-assisted deep research
  2. They craft prompts to generate a card front (portrait) and back (stats/info)
  3. Cards are submitted via Google Form and automatically sync to this gallery

A Note on the Art

Students used various AI image generators (ChatGPT/DALL-E, Midjourney, etc.) with varying results. We apologize in advance to any scientists whose AI-generated likeness missed the mark. We're working to improve the cards over time.
